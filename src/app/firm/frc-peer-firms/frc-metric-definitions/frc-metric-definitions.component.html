Key Metric Definitions:

  • Overall Spend: Total spend within the filtered dataset, including fee spend and AFA spend (note: if the ‘Include Expenses’ toggle is flipped on from the Bodhala landing page, this metric will include Expenses. By default, only Fees are included)
  • Total Matters: Count of all matters within the filtered dataset, regardless of matter status
  • Total Hours: Sum of all hours worked within the filtered dataset, regardless of matter status
  • Avg. Matter Cost: Sum of all TK Billing Costs (excluding expenses) on closed matters / Count of closed matters (note: this metric is never calculated using open matters)
  • Avg. Matter Hours: Sum of all hours worked on closed matters / Count of closed matters (note: this metric is never calculated using open matters)
  • Partner Hours Worked: % of hours worked by Partners, calculated as Sum of all hours worked by Partners / Sum of all Fee hours within the filtered dataset
  • Associate Hours Worked: % of hours worked by Associates, calculated as Sum of all hours worked by Associates / Sum of all Fee hours within the filtered dataset
  • Paralegal Hours Worked: % of hours worked by Paralegals, calculated as Sum of all hours worked by Paralegals / Sum of all Fee hours within the filtered dataset
  • Block Billing: Block billing occurs when a single attorney lumps several discrete tasks into one billing entry, which often results in inflated costs. Bodhala defines Block Billing as 4 or more hours on a single line item entry, and the Block Billing metric included here represents the % of total spend that was flagged as Block Billing
  • Avg. Partner Rate: Total Fee Spend worked by Partners / Total Hours worked by Partners
  • Avg. Associate Rate: Total Fee Spend worked by Associates / Total Hours worked by Associates
  • Avg. Blended Rate: Total Fee Spend worked by all timekeepers / Total Hours worked by all timekeepers (note: Blended Rate is a weighted average calculation, as it inherently factors in relative hours worked at different billing rates. This is a good way to quickly compare relative costs of working with different firms)
  • Avg. Paralegal Rate: Total Fee Spend worked by Paralegals / Total Hours worked by Paralegals
  • Avg. Legal Assistant Rate: Total Fee Spend worked by Legal Assistants / Total Hours worked by Legal Assistants
  • % of Work by Female Attys: Sum of all hours worked by timekeepers designated as Female / Total Hours worked within the filtered dataset
  • % of Work by Minority Attys: Sum of all hours worked by timekeepers designated as Minority (not White) / Total Hours worked within the filtered dataset
